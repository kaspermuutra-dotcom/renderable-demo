How to scan
your room
📍
Stand in the center
Pick a spot in the middle of the room and keep your feet planted there the whole time.
📱
Hold your phone at eye level
Keep the phone steady and horizontal. Point the camera straight ahead, not up or down.
🔄
Rotate slowly in a full circle
Turn your whole body slowly. The app auto-captures every 30°. Green checks appear as you go.
💡
Good lighting helps a lot
Turn on all the lights in the room. Dark or dim rooms produce blurry panoramas.
⚡ Don't rotate too fast — move slowly
💡 Make sure the room is well lit
📍 Keep your feet in one spot
📐 Hold phone level, not tilted
